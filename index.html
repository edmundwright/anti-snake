Anti-Snake

The snake likes apples, which is healthy. But he's a little too keen, and will eat them before they're ripe. Slow him down by building mazes!

  • Click and drag to build or destroy walls.
  • If you hold him back for too long the apple will go rotten, and its score will go down.
  • Take care to prevent the snake from getting trapped!
